üìä System Overview

SecurAccess is a modern, cloud-based access control system designed for properties with multiple units. It combines RFID keycard technology with real-time monitoring to provide secure, efficient access management for both residents and property managers.

System Components

  • RFID Reader Devices: IoT-enabled scanners at entry points
  • Electronic Lock Control: Servo-controlled magnetic locks
  • Live Camera Feed: Real-time CCTV monitoring with PIN verification
  • Cloud Database: Firebase for real-time data synchronization
  • Web Dashboard: Responsive interface for both tenants and landlords
  • Mobile-Friendly Design: Works on desktop, tablet, and smartphone

How It Works

When a tenant scans their RFID keycard at the reader:

  1. The reader transmits the card's unique ID to the system
  2. The system verifies the card against the database
  3. If valid, the lock activates and the door opens for 10 seconds
  4. Access is logged in real-time with timestamp and apartment details
  5. Both tenant and landlord can view the access record immediately

üîß Troubleshooting Guide

RFID & Lock Issues

Issue: Keycard not working / Access Denied

Possible Causes:

  • Keycard not registered in system
  • Keycard is deactivated or expired
  • RFID reader not powered on
  • Connection between reader and system interrupted

Solutions:

  1. Check if your keycard is registered (contact landlord)
  2. Verify card hasn't been deactivated
  3. Try scanning again after 5 seconds
  4. Check if other residents' cards work (to rule out reader issue)
  5. Contact landlord if problem persists
Issue: Lock not opening after successful scan

Possible Causes:

  • Servo motor malfunction
  • Magnetic lock issue
  • Electronic control system error

Solutions:

  1. Wait 30 seconds and try again
  2. Check if the lock shows any physical damage
  3. Note the time of the malfunction
  4. Contact landlord with time details

Dashboard & Login Issues

Issue: Cannot login / Invalid credentials

Solutions:

  1. Verify email address and password are correct (case-sensitive)
  2. Use "Forgot Password" to reset if needed
  3. Clear browser cache: Ctrl+Shift+Delete
  4. Try a different browser
  5. Ensure cookies are enabled in browser settings
  6. Contact system administrator if still unable to login
Issue: Dashboard not loading or "Loading..." stuck

Solutions:

  1. Hard refresh page: Ctrl+Shift+R (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)
  2. Check internet connection
  3. Wait 30 seconds for initial load
  4. Try a different browser
  5. Log out completely and log back in
  6. Check if Firebase service is operational

Camera Feed Issues

Issue: Camera feed shows "INSERT PIN" but won't connect

Solutions:

  1. Verify PIN is correct (case-sensitive)
  2. Ensure apartment is not "- - -" (select an apartment)
  3. Check if you have camera access permission (landlord must grant)
  4. Verify ESP32 camera module is powered on
  5. Check network connection to camera device
  6. Try from a different device to isolate issue

Real-Time Update Issues

Issue: New records not appearing in real-time

Solutions:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Ensure page is actively open (background tabs may pause)
  3. Try refreshing the page
  4. Check browser console for errors (F12 key)
  5. Disable browser extensions that might block connections
  6. Verify Firebase Realtime Database is active

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

Q: How does the RFID system work?

A: When you scan your keycard at the reader, it transmits your card ID to the system. The system verifies your identity and access permissions. If valid, the electronic lock opens for 10 seconds. All access attempts are logged automatically.

Q: Is my access data secure?

A: Yes. All data is encrypted using enterprise-grade security. You can only see your own records (tenants) or all records (landlords). Firebase provides backup, recovery, and compliance with data protection standards.

Q: What happens if I lose my keycard?

A: Submit a "Lost Keycard" request in the Keycard Management section. Your landlord will review and approve the request. You can request a replacement while your old card is deactivated to prevent unauthorized use.

Tenant-Specific Questions

Q: How do I see my access history?

A: Click "RFID Track Record" in your dashboard. You'll see your access statistics and a detailed table of all your scans. You can filter by date range to find specific entries.

Q: What does "üîì Unlocked" status mean?

A: This means your keycard scan was successful and the lock opened. The unlock status automatically transitions to üîí Locked after 10 seconds (the door closes).

Q: Why do I see repeated scans on the same day?

A: Each time you scan your card at the reader, it's logged as a separate entry. Multiple scans on the same day are normal if you enter and exit multiple times or if multiple people use the same reader.

Landlord-Specific Questions

Q: How do I approve pending requests?

A: Go to "Pending Approvals" or "Keycard Requests" in your dashboard. Review the request details and click the appropriate action button (Approve, Reject, or Request More Info). Tenants will be notified of your decision.

Q: Can I see which tenant accessed specific apartments?

A: Yes. Go to "RFID Access Log" and select the apartment from the filter buttons. You'll see all scans for that unit with tenant names, times, and status.

Q: How do I monitor cameras?

A: Click "Live Camera Feed" in your dashboard. Select an apartment and enter your admin PIN. The live camera stream will display. You must grant camera permissions to tenants who need access.

Q: What should I do if someone tries unauthorized access?

A: Check the RFID Access Log for denied attempts. Use the camera feed to verify if unauthorized activity is happening. Document the incident with timestamps. Consider deactivating suspicious cards or adjusting permissions.
