Privacy Notice

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. By filling out this form, you agree to the collection, use, and processing of the information you provide for the purposes stated below.

1. Purpose of Collection

The information collected through this form will be used solely for the management of secure access control, keycard administration, and facility security monitoring.

2. Information Collected

We may collect personal information such as:

  • Full name

  • Keycard access logs and usage data
  • Camera footage for security purposes

3. Data Sharing

Your information will be kept confidential and will not be shared with third parties, except as required by law or with your consent. Information may be shared with authorized landlords/administrators for management purposes only.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Data will be stored securely using Firebase cloud services in accordance with applicable data protection laws and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the stated purpose.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the information we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal data
  • Withdraw your consent at any time
  • Request deletion of your data
