The RFID Track Record feature allows you to view all your access attempts to your property in real-time. Track every entry and exit, see if attempts were successful or denied, and monitor when and where access occurred.

üí° Tip: This helps you identify any unauthorized attempts or verify that your keycard is working properly.

  • Detailed History: View all your RFID scans with full details
  • Statistics: Monitor total accesses, today's count, and last access time
  • Date Filtering: Filter records by specific dates
  • Status Indicators: Clearly see which attempts were allowed or denied

üöÄ How to Use

1 Login to Dashboard
Login to your tenant dashboard using your email and password.
2 Click RFID Track Record
In the left sidebar, click the "RFID Track Record" tab to view your access history.
3 View Your Statistics
See three quick stats: Total Access Count, Today's Access count, and your Last Access time.
4 Review Access History
Browse the table showing all your recent access attempts with timestamps, status, location, and duration.
5 Filter by Date (Optional)
Select a date in the filter, then click "Apply Filter" to see only records from that day. Click "Reset" to view all records.

‚ùì Common Scenarios

I see a "Denied" status - what should I do? ‚ñº

A "Denied" status means your keycard was not accepted. This could be because:

  • Your keycard is deactivated
  • Your keycard has expired
  • Your keycard is not properly configured
  • There's a temporary system issue

Action: Contact your landlord to check your keycard status.

Why is my access history empty? ‚ñº

Your history might be empty because:

  • You just signed up and haven't used your keycard yet
  • Your keycard has never been scanned at the reader
  • The records are older than what's shown (we keep recent records)

Action: Try scanning your keycard at the entrance and check back in a few seconds.

Can I see records from years ago? ‚ñº

The dashboard shows your recent access history. The exact duration depends on your system's configuration. However:

  • You can filter by date using the date picker
  • Very old records may have been archived
  • Contact your landlord if you need historical data
Is my access data secure? ‚ñº

Yes! Your access data is secured by:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit
  • Authentication: You must be logged in to view your data
  • Permissions: You can only see your own records (not other residents')
  • Landlord Access: Your landlord can view records for security purposes

üîß Troubleshooting

Issue: Records not loading or showing "Loading..."

Solution:

  1. Try refreshing the page (Ctrl+F5 or Cmd+Shift+R for hard refresh)
  2. Check your internet connection
  3. Log out and log back in
  4. Try a different browser
Issue: Real-time updates not working

Solution:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Make sure the page is still open (background tabs may pause)
  3. Refresh the page to reconnect
  4. Check your browser's privacy settings (some block real-time connections)
Issue: Getting permission errors

Solution:

  1. Verify you're logged in correctly
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies
  3. Log out completely and log back in
  4. Contact your landlord if the issue persists

üí° Tips & Best Practices

  • Use this feature to investigate any unauthorized access attempts
